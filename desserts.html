1. Chocolate Lava Cake

Ingredients:

  • 4 oz unsweetened chocolate
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 2 whole eggs
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 tbsp flour

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 425째F (220째C). Butter and flour four ramekins.
  2. Melt chocolate and butter together in a double boiler or microwave.
  3. Stir in sugar until combined. Whisk in eggs and yolks. Add vanilla and flour.
  4. Pour batter into ramekins and bake for 12-14 minutes.

2. Quick Berry Crumble

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups mixed berries (fresh or frozen)
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1/4 cup rolled oats
  • 1/4 cup cold butter (cubed)

Steps:

  1. Toss berries with lemon juice and place in a baking dish.
  2. In a separate bowl, mix brown sugar, flour, and oats. Cut in cold butter until crumbly.
  3. Sprinkle the topping evenly over the berries.
  4. Bake at 375째F (190째C) for 30-35 minutes until bubbly and golden.
